Our Recipe

Ingredients

  • Flour: 500g
  • Butter: 100g
  • Sugar: 150g
  • Cinnamon: 2 tbsp
  • Yeast: 1 tbsp
  • Milk: 250ml
  • Eggs: 2

Instructions

  1. Mix the dry ingredients together.
  2. Add the milk, butter, and eggs.
  3. Knead the dough and let it rise for 1 hour.
  4. Roll out the dough, add cinnamon sugar, and roll it up.
  5. Cut into rolls and bake at 180Â°C for 25 minutes.
