Our Recipe

Ingredients

  • Flour: 500g
  • Butter: 100g
  • Sugar: 150g
  • Cinnamon: 2 tbsp
  • Yeast: 1 tbsp
  • Milk: 250ml
  • Eggs: 2

Instructions

1. Mix the dry ingredients together

Combine the flour, sugar, cinnamon, and yeast in a large mixing bowl until evenly distributed. Wait for 8-10 minutes until it forms a bit of foam.

2. Add the milk, butter, and eggs

Pour warm milk, melted butter, and beaten eggs into the mixture. Knead the dough for 10 minutes until smooth and elastic. Cover and let rise in a warm place for 1 hour.

3. Roll out the dough

Roll the dough into a rectangular shape. Spread a layer of softened butter, then sprinkle generously with a mix of cinnamon and sugar.

4. Roll up and cut

Roll the dough tightly from one side to form a log. Slice into even pieces, about 2 inches thick.

5. Bake and enjoy

Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F). Place rolls onto a baking tray lined with parchment paper. Bake for 25 minutes or until golden brown. Let cool slightly before serving.
